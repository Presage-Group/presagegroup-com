"Rapid Assessment|Confidential, rapid assessment of the entire mental health ecosystem, including common mental illnesses, resilience, coping ability, perceived stigma, and aviation-specific stressors.|circle-check"
"Organizational Reports|Organizational reports of mental health risk and trends for operational groups and divisions (with demographic breakdown by age, rank, role, experience, etc.)|bar-chart-2"
"Risk Assessment|Links pilots' mental health to degradation in situational awareness, decision making, cognitive function, and overall performance.|shield-alert"
